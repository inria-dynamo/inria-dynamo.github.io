Istituto Italiano di Tecnologia

The Computational Statistics and Machine Learning (CSML) group conducts research at the intersection of machine learning theory and algorithm design, grounded in mathematics and statistics, with particular emphasis on empirical processes and numerical optimization.

  • Vladimir Kostic, Researcher (IIT, Italy), Associate Professor (Univ. Novi Sad, Serbia).
    numerical computing, statistical learning theory, Markov transfer operators
  • Massimiliano Pontil, Senior Researcher (IIT, Italy), Professor (UCL, UK), Head of CSML.
    statistical learning theory, neural operators, dynamical systems
  • Saverio Salzo, Associate Professor (La Sapienza, Italy), Visiting Scientist (IIT, Italy).
    numerical optimization

Non-Permanent Staff: Riccardo Grazzi (Postdoc), Erfan Mirzaei (PhD Student), Pietro Novelli (Postdoc).
